Value Quality, not Quantity

Contributions are charged by size to incentivize brevity and allow people to "put their money where their mouth is."

Authors are rewarded when other people respond to their content; higher quality, more responses, deeper discussions, bigger yields.

Starve the Trolls

By only responding to messages that are of value to the community, an incentive is created for high-quality content while low-quality content that doesn't generate a response results in a net loss.

If a troll replies to your content, ignore them and enjoy the points you received from them!
